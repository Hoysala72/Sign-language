About SignLangAI

SignLangAI is an open-source project dedicated to breaking communication barriers for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community. Using state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning, our platform recognizes American Sign Language (ASL) letters and common phrases in real time through your webcam.

  • Real-time sign language recognition
  • Personalized detection history and statistics
  • Community-driven dataset expansion
  • Modern, accessible, and mobile-friendly design

Our mission is to make sign language technology accessible to everyone, everywhere. We welcome contributions and feedback from the community!
